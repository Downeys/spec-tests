# Business Strategy Planner

## What This Is

A personal AI agent system that helps me research, brainstorm, define, and document investor-grade business plans (strategy, marketing, financials) — guided by a hybrid memory architecture pattern (Karpathy wiki + OneBrain research warehouse) that I'm proving out as a reusable approach. Chat-first UI; the agent populates an Obsidian wiki and a research warehouse as we work, with every strategic decision tagged for confidence and traceable to evidence.

## Core Value

Every strategic claim in the wiki is connected to research-backed evidence with explicit confidence — the system makes a business plan defensible by construction.

## Context

**Memory architecture pattern (under test, single pattern — Karpathy wiki + OneBrain *hybrid* per Nate B Jones interpretation):**

Three layers with a directional write path: research enters OneBrain first, the wiki is a *compiled view* on top.

- **CLAUDE.md** — defines the agent's identity and behavioral principles (be critical, treat statements as hypotheses, evidence-first reasoning).
- **OneBrain (structured DB, source of truth)** — Postgres-backed research warehouse. Every fact, source, finding, decision, hypothesis is stored as a tagged, categorized, queryable row with confidence + provenance. Multi-agent safe (concurrent writes without merge conflicts). Scales beyond the wiki's natural ceiling.
- **Compilation agent** — distinct synthesis agent (scheduled and on-demand) that reads OneBrain and emits/updates the wiki. Drives the wiki refresh; preserves contradictions rather than smoothing them away.
- **Karpathy wiki (compiled view, Obsidian-flavored markdown vault)** — Maps the business model and strategic frameworks, the connections between them, and the research backing each decision. Generated by the compilation agent, not hand-edited or directly written from chat. Browsed in Obsidian; surfaced as markdown chunks in the chat UI.

**References:**
- Karpathy gist: https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f → saved at `.planning/inputs/karpathy-llm-wiki-gist.md`
- Nate B Jones hybrid interpretation transcript → saved at `.planning/inputs/nate-b-jones-hybrid-transcript.md`

**Why hybrid (vs. either pure pattern):** pure-wiki breaks at scale and on multi-agent writes; pure-DB has weak narrative/cross-reference/contradiction-surfacing. Hybrid keeps the structured DB as durable truth while still producing the navigable narrative artifact. Wiki claims trace back to specific OneBrain rows (evidence linking).

**Critical/hypothesis behavior expressed in two layers:**
- In chat — agent verbally pushes back, asks for evidence, surfaces counter-arguments.
- In wiki — every node carries a confidence level and a link to evidence in OneBrain.

**v1 milestone scope:**
A wiki populated with strategy/marketing/JTBD/positioning docs, all interconnected, all backed by research stored in OneBrain. Financial *analysis* is included as research evidence. Full financial *projections* and the polish required for investor scrutiny come in later iterations toward the north star.

**North star (multi-iteration):**
A business plan with financial projections that pass investor scrutiny. v1 lays the strategic + research foundation; later iterations build the financial model on top.

**Strategic framework families in scope (across the lifecycle):**
- Classical positioning (SWOT, STP, 4Ps, Porter's 5 Forces)
- Brand strategy (brand pyramid, positioning statement, voice/tone, messaging architecture)
- JTBD / customer (jobs-to-be-done, customer journey maps, ICPs, persona docs)

**Open architectural decisions (defer to research):**
- Multi-agent topology — coordinator with sub-agents vs agent-team with peer-to-peer communication
- Orchestration framework — LangChain/LangGraph vs Anthropic Agent SDK
- Web search tool — Tavily or equivalent

**Testing context:**
The Karpathy wiki + OneBrain hybrid is the only memory pattern being implemented; the goal is to build it well, not to compare it against alternatives.
