# Business-Planning Research Agent

A personal-use, hypothesis-driven research agent that guides the user in researching, brainstorming, defining, and documenting a full business plan including comprehensive marketing strategy and financial projections. The agent is critical of every finding and decision; it treats every statement as a hypothesis that needs testing, supports or refutes **Hypotheses** with cited **Claims**, and renders **Business Plans**, **Marketing Plans**, and **Financial Projections** at read-time from a web of **Strategic Frameworks**.
